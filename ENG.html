International School of English

W.I.S.E. is a British English school in Wakayama City. It aims to build an English-speaking community in the city through a variety of English classes, activities, workshops and events. Children from 4yrs to adults of all abilities and backgrounds are welcome to have an experience of choice.

About Rob Sensei

Robert is an experienced native British English teacher. After traveling around the world in 2009, he has taught all over the world as well as at a UK Primary School. Robert has a First Class Honours Degree in Geography and a Master’s degree in Environmental Science. In addition, Robert also has 10 years’ experience in Project, Operations and Business Management.

Rob Sensei has many hobbies and interests, which makes classes enjoyable and interesting. Amongst these are: business, traveling, environmental conservation, fishing, health, fitness, sports and reading. Several of these interests have been studied and certified so that Rob Sensei can professionally deliver the content to students in his classes.
