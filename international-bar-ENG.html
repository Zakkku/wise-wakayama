International Bar and Conversation Cafe

Every Friday from 20:00 – 23:00 W.I.S.E. hosts a free-entry international conversation bar and cafe. Here you can practice your English communication skills with people from all over the world, whilst drinking and snacking ... from our 100% international menu. The venue is non-smoking and adults and children are welcome to join too! Join our regular events, from lively poker nights to creative art classes.
