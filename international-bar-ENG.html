International Bar and Conversation Cafe

Welcome to our International Bar & Conversation Cafe! Discover a selection of alcohols from every corner of the globe while savoring delicious snacks that promise a journey of flavors. But it's more than just a feast for your palate; it's a hub for language enthusiasts to enjoy conversation, bond over games, and share laughs with people from diverse cultures. Set in a casual, family-friendly, and non-smoking ambiance, we ensure every guest feels at home. And for those seeking a dash of extra fun, join our regular events, from lively poker nights to creative art classes. Dive into an experience that's truly global, right here in Wakayama!
